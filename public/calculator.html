How this works

Concentration = vial amount (mg) ÷ bacteriostatic water added (ml). Draw volume = target dose ÷ concentration. U-100 insulin syringe units = draw volume (ml) × 100. This is arithmetic only, it does not know your specific vial's real fill amount, purity, or condition.
