About Highland Peptides

Small supplier,
careful process.

We're a research-use peptide supplier built around a simple idea: verify who's buying, keep the paperwork straight, and ship reliably.

What we actually do

Every account on this site is reviewed by a person before it's approved. There is no guest checkout and no way to buy without going through that review. We think that manual step matters more than any badge or claim we could put on the homepage: it's the actual mechanism that keeps this a business-to-business, research-use catalog rather than a walk-up storefront.

Sourcing

Our catalog is sourced from an established peptide manufacturer. A Certificate of Analysis (COA) confirming identity and purity is available on request for any product lot before you order, just ask. We deliberately keep certain items off this catalog altogether when they carry prescription-only status or documented FDA safety flags; that list, and our reasoning, is available on request.

Shipping

We ship from California via tracked common carrier. All products are sold strictly for laboratory and research use, not for human or veterinary consumption, injection, or administration of any kind. Full terms are shown at signup.
