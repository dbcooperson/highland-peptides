Handling & Reconstitution

  • Reconstitute using sterile bacteriostatic water.
  • Swirl gently, do not shake, until fully dissolved.
  • Handle only within a controlled laboratory environment by qualified personnel.
  • Use our Peptide Calculator for reconstitution math.

Storage

  • Store lyophilized peptide cool, dry, and protected from light.
  • Refrigerate reconstituted solutions.
  • Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles.
